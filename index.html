Crystal Collector

At the start of the game you will be given a random number to match.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score exceeds the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you. Once you click on a crystal, the hidden value will be added to the total score.

Every time the game starts you'll get a new random number to match and the value of each crystal will randomly change.
